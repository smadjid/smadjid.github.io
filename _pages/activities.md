## Event organization

### EIAH 2023 — 11th Conference on Computer Environments for Human Learning  
**Workshop Co-Chair & Session Chair**

Member of the program and organizing committees of [EIAH 2023](https://eiah2023.sciencesconf.org) (IMT Atlantique, Brest, June 12–16, 2023).

- Co-Chair of the Workshops track: call coordination, proposal evaluation, and scientific structuring of the workshop program.  
- Chair of the session *“Pedagogy and Ludicization.”*


### CSEDU 2023 — International Conference on Computer Supported Education  
**Session Chair — Virtual & Augmented Learning Environments**

Chair of the session on [Virtual and Augmented Learning Environments](https://www.insticc.org/node/TechnicalProgram/csedu/2023/session/P21938) at [CSEDU 2023](https://csedu.scitevents.org/?y=2023).

Responsibilities included session moderation, scientific discussion facilitation, and coordination of paper presentations on immersive learning systems.


### RJC-EIAH 2022  
**Workshop Co-Organizer — Design and Evaluation of Learning Dashboards**

Co-organization of the workshop  
[Design and Evaluation of Learning Dashboards](https://padlad.github.io/RJC-EIAH2022/)  
(RJC-EIAH 2022, Lille, May 9–11).

Focus: design methods, evaluation frameworks, and quality processes for learning analytics dashboards.  
This initiative contributed to the structuring of the ATIEF working group on the topic.


### EIAH 2021  
**Workshop Co-Organizer — Participatory Design of Learning Dashboards**

Co-organization of the workshop  
[Participatory Design Tool for Learning Dashboards](https://padlad.github.io/EIAH2021/)  
(EIAH 2021, Fribourg, June 7–11).

The workshop explored participatory approaches to dashboard design and decision-support modeling.


### CERIST Challenge Days (2018–2019)  
**General Chair**

President of the national [CERIST Challenge Days](http://challenge.cerist.dz/) innovation competition (Algiers).

- 2018 edition — June 19–21  
- 2019 edition — October 15–17  

Competition dedicated to AI and technology projects with societal impact.

## Scientific community activities

### ATIEF Working Group — Learning Analytics Dashboards (GT TBA)  
**Member**

Active contributor to the [GT TBA](http://www.atief.fr/content/gt-tba) working group (ATIEF).

Research focus:

- Design and evaluation of learning analytics dashboards  
- Indicator quality and interpretability  
- Reflective and documentation practices in learning contexts  


### PaDLAD Working Group  
**Founding Member — Research & Development**

Co-initiator of the [PaDLAD working group](https://padlad.github.io), dedicated to participatory design of learning analytics dashboards.

Activities include:

- Method and tool development  
- Participatory workshops  
- Stakeholder co-design processes  
- Dissemination through publications and community events  


### CERIST Digital Library  
**Executive Member — Development & Administration**

Contribution to the development and management of the CERIST institutional repository, providing access to scientific publications, reports, and academic resources.
